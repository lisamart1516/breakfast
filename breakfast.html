What are we making: 2 EGGS AND TURKEY BACON BREAKFAST

By: Lisa Martinez

Sometimes when my mom is not home and is unable to cook, I tend to make a meal out of the simplest and easy-to-grab ingredients.
This is the quickest breakfast option and you only need a few ingredients

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 eggs
  • one teaspoon of salt
  • one teaspoon of pepper
  • one and a half tablespoons of butter
  • handful of spinach leaves
  • 4 strips of Oscar Mayers Turkey Bacon

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Turn on the skillet medium-to-low temperature for eggs
  2. Put one and a half tablespoons of butter in the skillet to melt
  3. Add the 2 eggs to skillet
  4. Add the teaspoon of salt
  5. Add the teaspoon of pepper
  6. Stir eggs till they begin to blend and scramble
  7. Turn on second skillet for the turkey bacon
  8. Lay the 4 strips of turkey bacon on the skillet
  9. turn bacon strips until they are dark in color-burgundyish red- on both sides
  10. Reduce heat, plate and serve
ENJOY!
